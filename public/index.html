Our Story

Crafting Quality.
Building Trust.

KATYA EXIM is a growing export business specializing in high-quality textile products — including towels, napkins, bedsheets, pillow covers, shawls, stoles, and scarves. Based in New Delhi, we are committed to delivering products that combine comfort, durability, and elegant design.

We work closely with trusted manufacturers to ensure consistent quality and timely delivery for our international clients. Our focus is on building long-term relationships through transparency, competitive pricing, and reliable service.

At Katya EXIM, we aim to bring the richness of Indian textiles to global markets while meeting the evolving needs of customers worldwide.
